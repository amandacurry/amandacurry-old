Hello World

Fri 20 November 2015 | welcome, introduction

Welcome to my blog! Throughout my life I have been told countless times to keep a work journal as good way to look back at challenges I overcame and see what I had achieved; but keeping a physical journal seems a bit ironic when I work in computing. A blog, on the other hand, is an opportunity for me to document my projects not just for myself but also anyone else who has come across the same problems.

How I built this blog

I am a real fan of simplicity so I really liked the idea of a static website. This blog was built using Pelican http://blog.getpelican.com/, a python-based static blog generator. You can read up on why you would want a static blog .

You can then create your own template to make your blog look exactly as you want. Here I have incorporated bootstrap. There are plenty of tutorials online.
